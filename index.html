Quick Start

  • Dashboard opens straight to all leads — no login required
  • Use the role buttons in the header to switch your view
  • Click any lead in the sidebar to open it
  • Use Move to step to advance a lead through the pipeline
  • Click Assign Rep to assign a rep and auto-draft a notification email
  • CSV Import automatically skips leads already in the system — re-uploading is safe
  • Settings password: pipeline2024

🛡 Duplicate Protection

CSV Import checks by name + email before inserting. Leads already in the system are automatically skipped — even if you upload the same file again. Leads with an assigned rep or pipeline progress are never overwritten.
