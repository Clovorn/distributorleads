Distributor Deal Portal — Help & Support

Everything you need to manage leads, assign reps, and move deals through the pipeline

📝 Getting Started

The Distributor Deal Portal tracks every distributor lead through the Java Select Program — from the first contact all the way to a completed installation. Here's how to get up and running quickly.

🤝 How to Assign a Sales Rep to a Lead

1
Select a lead from the left sidebar

Click any lead card to open it in the main panel.

2
Open the Move to Step dropdown

In the "Move to step" box, select 👤 Assign Rep from the dropdown.

3
Click the green Advance button

The Assign Rep modal will appear automatically.

4
Select the Sales Rep

Choose the rep from the dropdown. All active reps are listed alphabetically.

5
Enter the rep's email address

Type or paste their email. The notification email body is pre-filled with all customer information.

6
Click "Copy email & assign"

The rep is saved to the lead, the pipeline advances, and the email is copied to your clipboard. Paste it into your email client to send.

📈 How to Advance a Lead Through the Pipeline

1
Select the lead

Click the lead in the sidebar to open it.

2
Use the Move to Step dropdown

Select the step you want to move to, then click Move →. Or click the green Advance → button to go to the very next step.

3
Add a note (optional)

A note modal will appear. You can add context about this step — what was discussed, documents sent, next actions, etc. Notes are saved to the activity log.

4
Confirm

Click Save & advance. The pipeline updates immediately.

🔍 How to Filter and Search Leads

The Filter & Sort panel at the top lets you narrow down the lead list:

  • Search — type any name, store, email, phone, or warehouse to instantly filter
  • Sales Rep — filter all leads assigned to a specific rep
  • Status — show only Active, Won, or Lost leads
  • Route — filter by Sales Rep Route or Leasing/Finance Route
  • Program — filter by Java Select program type
  • More filters — expand for Interest, Warehouse, Lead Type, and Date Range

You can also click any of the 5 metric cards at the top (Total, Needs Assigned, Assigned, Won, Lost) to instantly filter the sidebar.

📊 How to Use the Rep Report

1
Click "Rep Report" in the toolbar

Opens a full report of all leads grouped by assigned sales rep.

2
Filter by a specific rep

Use the dropdown at the top of the report to view only one rep's leads.

3
Edit leads directly from the report

Click the Edit button on any row to update lead details without leaving the report.

📥 How to Import Leads from Jotform CSV

1
Export from Jotform

Go to ronnoco.jotform.com → your form → Tables → ··· → Export as CSV.

2
Click "↑ Import CSV" in the toolbar

The import modal will open.

3
Select your CSV file

A preview will confirm how many submissions were found.

4
Click Import

New leads are added. Duplicate submissions (same Jotform ID) are automatically skipped.

👥 Understanding User Roles

The portal has four roles. Switch between them using the buttons in the top header. Each role controls what actions are available.

👑 Admin

  • Full access to all leads, all actions
  • Can assign reps, advance pipeline, mark lost, delete leads
  • Access to Settings page (password required)
  • Can manage sales reps list, change admin password
  • Can import CSV files and sync Jotform
  • Can view all rep reports

🏗 Director

  • Full visibility into all leads and pipeline activity
  • Can assign reps and advance the pipeline
  • Can mark leads as lost
  • Can delete leads
  • No access to Settings page

🚚 Dist Rep (Distributor Rep)

  • Sees leads relevant to the distributor workflow
  • Can view New Leads and Sales Route leads
  • Limited pipeline advancement ability
  • No access to Settings or delete functions

👔 Ronnoco Rep

  • Can view all leads
  • Can advance the pipeline on leads assigned to them
  • Can mark leads as lost if assigned
  • No access to Settings or delete functions

👋 Welcome to the Distributor Deal Portal

Think of this portal as your command center for the Java Select Program — the place where every lead that comes in through Jotform or manual entry gets tracked, assigned, nurtured, and celebrated when it closes. Nothing falls through the cracks here.

The portal is built around a simple idea: every potential customer is on a journey, and your job is to guide them from New Lead all the way to Complete. The pipeline is your roadmap.

🖥 The Layout at a Glance

  • Metric Cards (top) — Five clickable tiles showing Total Leads, Needs Assigned, Assigned, Won, and Lost. Click any card to instantly filter the sidebar to that group.
  • Filter & Sort Panel — Search by name, rep, or anything. Filter by status, route, program, and more. Secondary filters hide behind "More filters" to keep things tidy.
  • Left Sidebar — Your scrollable list of leads. Each card shows the customer name, assigned rep (blue badge), pipeline step, and submission date. Red badge = needs a rep assigned.
  • Main Panel (right) — Click any lead card to open its full detail here. This is where you manage everything: route, pipeline, rep assignment, editable fields, submission data, and the activity log.

🎭 Roles

Use the role buttons in the header to switch your view. Each role shows a different perspective on the same data:

  • Admin — Full access. Create, edit, delete, advance, assign, mark lost. The keys to the kingdom.
  • Director — Same as Admin but focused on oversight. Full pipeline control.
  • Dist Rep — Distributor rep view. Sees active Sales Route leads only (not leasing).
  • Ronnoco Rep — Sales rep view. Can advance leads that are assigned to a rep.

➕ Adding a New Lead Manually

1
Click "+ New lead" in the toolbar

A form pops up asking for customer name, store, email, phone, distributor rep, warehouse, program, and customer interest.

2
Fill in what you know

Only the customer name is required. Fill in as much as you have — you can always edit it later.

3
Click "Create lead"

The lead appears instantly in the sidebar as a New Lead, ready to be assigned.

📤 Importing from Jotform CSV

1
Export from Jotform

In Jotform Tables, click the ··· menu → Export as CSV.

2
Click "↑ Import CSV" in the toolbar

Upload the CSV file. The portal will show you how many submissions it found.

3
Click "Import"

New leads are added. Duplicates (matched by submission ID) are automatically skipped — no double-entries.

✏️ Editing a Lead

Open any lead and scroll down to the Editable fields section. You can update the customer name, DBA, email, phone, legal name, address, warehouse, customer distributor number, and notes. Hit Save changes when done. Every save is logged in the activity log at the bottom of the lead.

🗑 Deleting a Lead

Admin and Director roles see a red Delete button in the top-right of the lead panel. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone — use it carefully. Marking a lead as Lost instead of deleting it is usually the better move, since it keeps the history.

🗺 The Two Routes

Every lead follows one of two routes depending on how the customer wants to acquire their equipment:

  • Sales Rep Route — For customers buying outright or using a loaned setup. 13 steps from New Lead to Complete.
  • Leasing/Finance Route — For customers leasing or financing through Smarter Equipment Finance. 8 steps from New Lead to Deal Funded.

You can switch a lead's route at any time using the Route toggle buttons at the top of the lead panel.

⚡ Advancing a Lead

1
Use the "Move to step" dropdown

Select any step from the dropdown and click Move → to jump directly to it. A note modal appears — add context about why you're moving the lead.

2
Or use the green Advance button

The green button below the pipeline always advances to the very next step. For the Assign Rep step, it opens the rep assignment modal instead.

3
Add a note (optional but encouraged)

Every step change prompts you for a note. These notes are saved in the activity log and visible to everyone working the lead.

💸 Leasing & Financing — The Credit Application

If a customer wants to lease or finance, the credit application is the very next step after assigning a rep. When you assign a rep to a leasing lead, the notification email pre-fills with the Smarter Equipment Finance link:

► Smarter Equipment Finance — Credit Application:
https://signnow.com/s/YKsq02bU

The rep should copy this link and send it to the customer immediately after being assigned. The sooner it's submitted, the faster the deal moves.

🏆 Won & ✗ Lost

Won: Certain steps (Customer Won, Complete, Deal Funded) automatically mark the lead as Won when you advance to them.

Lost: Click the red "✗ Mark lost" button and provide a reason. The lead dims in the sidebar and is hidden by default — click "Show lost" in the toolbar to bring lost leads back into view. Lost leads remain in the database for reporting.

👤 Assigning a Sales Rep

Assigning a rep is a key step in the pipeline — it triggers an email notification to the rep with everything they need to get started.

1
Open the lead and click the green Advance button

If the lead is on New Lead, the Advance button says "Advance → 👤 Assign Rep". Click it.

2
Select the rep from the dropdown

All active sales reps are listed. Pick the right one for this territory and program.

3
Enter the rep's email address

The notification email body pre-fills with all customer info, deal details, distributor info, notes, the portal link, and the Smarter Equipment Finance link for leasing deals.

4
Click "Copy email & assign"

The rep is saved to the lead, the pipeline advances to Assign Rep, and the email is copied to your clipboard. Paste it into your email client and send. Direct email sending is coming soon.

📊 Rep Report

Click the 📊 Rep Report button in the toolbar for a full breakdown of every lead by sales rep — total, won, lost, and active counts, plus a table of each rep's leads with current step and status. Use the dropdown at the top of the report to filter by a single rep. You can also edit leads directly from the report.

⚙️ Managing the Rep List

Go to Settings (Admin password required) → Sales Reps section to add new reps, deactivate reps who are no longer active, reactivate them, or delete them. Deactivated reps are hidden from assignment dropdowns but their leads remain in the system.

🔍 Search

The search bar matches across: customer name, store/DBA, email, phone, assigned rep, distributor rep, warehouse, and program. Just start typing and the sidebar updates instantly. It's not picky about capitalization.

📌 Metric Card Quick Filters

Click any of the five metric cards at the top to instantly filter the sidebar:

  • Total Leads — Show everything
  • Needs Assigned — Leads with no rep yet (your priority queue)
  • Assigned — Leads with a rep actively working them
  • Won — Closed deals 🏆
  • Lost — Lost deals (click again to deactivate the filter)

🗂 Filter Panel

The filter panel has two tiers:

  • Primary filters (always visible) — Sales Rep, Status, Route, Program
  • More filters (click to expand) — Interest, Warehouse, Lead Type (tradeshow vs. non-tradeshow), Date range

Active filters show a count badge in the panel header. Click Clear all to reset everything at once.

📅 Sorting

Use the Sort dropdown to order leads by: Submission date (newest or oldest first), Name A→Z, Sales rep A→Z, Distributor rep A→Z, Warehouse A→Z, or Pipeline step. The sort combines with any active filters.
