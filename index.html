Quick Start

  • Dashboard opens straight to all leads — no login required
  • Use the role buttons in the header to switch your view
  • Click any lead in the sidebar to open it
  • Use Move to step to advance a lead through the pipeline
  • Click Assign Rep to assign a rep and auto-draft a notification email
