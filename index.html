User Guide & Help Center · v2.0

👋 Welcome to the Distributor Deal Portal

Your command center for the Java Select & Distributor Programs. Every lead that comes in gets tracked, assigned, nurtured, and celebrated when it closes. Nothing falls through the cracks.

🎭 Roles

  • Admin — Full access including User Management
  • Director — Full access, pipeline control, no User Management
  • Sales Rep — Sees only leads assigned to them
  • Distributor — Sees only leads for their accounts

➕ Adding a Lead

Click + New lead in the toolbar. Fill in customer name, store, email, phone, and program details. Click Create lead.

📤 Importing CSV

Click ↑ Import CSV, select the Distributor Program, upload the Jotform export CSV. Duplicates are skipped automatically.

🗺 Two Routes

  • Sales Rep Route — Buying/loaned equipment. 13 steps.
  • Leasing/Finance Route — Leasing or financing. 8 steps.

⚡ Advancing

Use the Move to step dropdown and click Move →. Or click the green Advance button. For Assign Rep, a modal opens to select the rep and copy the notification email.

👤 Assigning a Rep

1
Click Advance → Assign Rep

Opens the rep assignment modal

2
Select rep, enter email

Email pre-fills with all customer details

3
Click Copy email & assign

Rep saved, pipeline advances, email copied to clipboard

🔍 Search

Searches name, store, email, phone, rep, warehouse, and program.

📌 Quick Filters

Click the metric cards at the top to filter by Total, Needs Assigned, Assigned, Won, or Lost.

Check active filters — click Clear all to reset, or click Total Leads metric card.
When assigning a rep to a leasing lead, the notification email pre-fills with the Smarter Equipment Finance link: https://signnow.com/s/YKsq02bU
Lost keeps the lead in history. Deleted removes it permanently. Always prefer Lost.
