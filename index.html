Lead Pipeline Stages
  1. Lead Received — Lead came in from a tradeshow, webhook, or CSV import.
  2. Awaiting Director — Admin needs to route the lead to a Director.
  3. Awaiting Rep Assignment — Director needs to assign one of their Sales Reps.
  4. Assigned to Rep — Rep has been assigned but has not yet made first contact.
  5. Customer Contacted — Rep made first real contact (call, email, in-person).
  6. In Follow-up — Ongoing back-and-forth until the deal is Won or Lost.

Terminal: Won or Lost. A Won lead converts to a deal in the Ronnoco Deal Builder.

User Roles & Permissions
  • Admin — Sees all leads. Routes new leads to Directors. Manages reps, directors, sync settings.
  • Director — Sees leads routed to them by Admin, plus their team's active leads. Assigns Sales Reps from their team.
  • Distributor — Sees only leads from their program (e.g. Java Select). Submits new leads, tracks status, and can view rep activity notes.
  • Ronnoco Rep — Sees only leads assigned to them. Logs follow-ups, marks won/lost, edits customer contact info.
